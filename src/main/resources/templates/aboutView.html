About Us

Meet our team! Learn about the brains, brawns, and the beautes behind the amazing World Trekking Co. We're a very diverse group, with a crazy array of personalities, brilliant list of talents, and especially, the most unique designs of nature.

Yuli

Yuli is the truly the superhero of the superheroes. She does everything every superhero that ever existed can do, aaannnnd basketweaves.

Randy

Randy is the sweetest bee with the bestest honey in the world. But he has a mischievous streak. He'll trap you with that sticky honey and grill you if you get too close.

Jordan

Jordan is everyone's favorite one-eyed, one-legged pirate ever. He may look scary on the outside, but he has the softest, squishiest heart. He really just wears that patch to cover the eye from which he cries easily.

Kouric

Kouric is the best friend Randy wished he had. Energetic and optimistic, he too has a mischievous streak. Aside from catching flies in his spare time, his favorite activity is bouncing.

Kit

Kit is just a goofy penguin. Not much else to her. Really. She spends a lot of time wishing she could fly. And eating krill, squid, and crabs.
